How does Crop Prediction work?

Crop Prediction uses machine learning algorithms to analyze various factors that affect crop growth, such as weather conditions, soil properties, and crop type. The algorithms then use this information to predict the most suitable crop to grow in a given location and time.

What are the benefits of Crop Prediction?

Crop Prediction can help farmers to:

  • Maximize crop yield and profits
  • Reduce crop losses due to unfavorable conditions
  • Make informed decisions about crop selection and management

What data is used for Crop Prediction?

Crop Prediction uses various sources of data, such as:

  • Historical weather data
  • Soil surveys and analysis
  • Crop growth and yield data
  • Field observations and measurements

Data Source
